FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries about our products and services.

All our energy storage products use Lithium Iron Phosphate (LFP) chemistry, known for its exceptional safety, long cycle life (6000–8000 cycles), and thermal stability. We also offer semi-solid LFP technology for select products, which provides even greater safety — the electrolyte is solidified within a crystal lattice, eliminating leakage and explosion risks.

Our products are certified under IEC62619, EN61000-6-1, EN61000-3, RoHS, UN38.3, and GB/T36276. We also hold ISO quality management certifications. For outdoor enclosures, we achieve IP54/IP55 ingress protection and C3/C4/C5 corrosion resistance ratings.

All energy storage products come with a standard 5-year warranty. Our batteries are designed for 6000–8000 cycles at 0.5P (5%–95% DOD, 70% SOH), with an annual degradation rate of less than 3%.

Yes, we provide OEM and ODM services. Our R&D team can customize products to your specifications, including branding, technical parameters, and system configuration.

MOQ varies by product category. For sample orders and trial projects, we are flexible. Please contact our sales team with your requirements for a specific quotation.

Shipping time depends on the product type, order quantity, and destination. Standard lead time for production is 4–8 weeks. We ship via sea freight (FOB/CIF), with air freight available for urgent orders. We have experience delivering to Asia, Africa, the Middle East, and Europe.
